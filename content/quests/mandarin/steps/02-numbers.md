# Day 2: Numbers

## Learning Numbers 1-10

Numbers are essential for everyday communication. Today you'll master numbers 1-10 in Mandarin.

## Practice with Flashcards

Each flashcard shows a Chinese number character. Practice recognizing and pronouncing each number.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Number System

Chinese numbers are straightforward:
- Each number has a single character
- Numbers combine logically (e.g., 十一 = 10 + 1 = 11)
- Once you learn 1-10, you can build larger numbers

## Tips

- **Write them out** - Practice writing the characters
- **Count objects** - Count things around you in Chinese
- **Practice pronunciation** - Pay attention to tones
- **Use in context** - Try saying "I have [number] apples"
