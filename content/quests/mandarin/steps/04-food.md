# Day 4: Food

## Learning Food Vocabulary

Food vocabulary is essential for daily life. Learn words for common foods and drinks, plus the verbs "to eat" and "to drink."

## Practice with Flashcards

Each card shows a food item or action. Practice saying what you like to eat and drink!

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Food Categories

You'll learn:
- **Drinks**: water, tea
- **Staples**: rice, noodles
- **Food types**: vegetables, meat, fruit
- **Actions**: to eat, to drink

## Tips

- **Practice with meals** - Name foods as you eat
- **Combine with numbers** - "I want two apples" (我要两个苹果)
- **Learn preferences** - "I like..." (我喜欢...)
- **Order food** - Use these words in restaurants

## Ready to Practice?

Food vocabulary is practical and fun! Use the flashcards to learn these essential words for eating and drinking.
