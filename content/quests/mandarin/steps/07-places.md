# Day 7: Places

## Learning Place Vocabulary

Learn words for common places you visit. These are essential for giving directions and talking about where you go.

## Practice with Flashcards

Each card shows a place name. Practice saying where you go and what you do at each place.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Common Places

You'll learn:
- **Education**: school, library
- **Services**: store, hospital, bank, restaurant
- **Recreation**: park
- **Transportation**: station
- **Location**: here

## Tips

- **Combine with actions** - "I go to school" (我去学校)
- **Practice directions** - "Where is the bank?" (银行在哪里?)
- **Use with time** - "Today I go to the park" (今天我去公园)
- **Real-world practice** - Name places as you visit them

## Ready to Practice?

Place vocabulary helps you navigate and talk about your daily activities. Use the flashcards to learn these essential location words!
