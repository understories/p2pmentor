# Day 8: Actions

## Learning Action Verbs

Verbs are the action words that make sentences work. Learn common verbs for daily activities.

## Practice with Flashcards

Each card shows an action verb. Practice forming sentences like "I [verb]" (我[verb]).

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Action Categories

You'll learn:
- **Movement**: go, come
- **Senses**: look, listen, speak
- **Learning**: read, write, study
- **Work**: work, rest

## Tips

- **Form simple sentences** - "I study" (我学习)
- **Combine with places** - "I go to school" (我去学校)
- **Practice daily** - Describe what you do each day
- **Use with time** - "Today I work" (今天我工作)

## Ready to Practice?

Action verbs bring your vocabulary to life! Use them to describe what you do every day. Start with the flashcards below.
