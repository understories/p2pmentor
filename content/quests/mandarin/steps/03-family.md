# Day 3: Family

## Learning Family Vocabulary

Family is central to Chinese culture. Today you'll learn words for family members.

## Practice with Flashcards

Learn the Chinese words for family members. Notice how some words are repeated syllables (爸爸, 妈妈).

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Family Structure

Chinese has specific terms for:
- Older vs younger siblings (哥哥/弟弟, 姐姐/妹妹)
- Different family relationships
- Extended family concepts

## Cultural Note

In Chinese culture, family relationships are very important. The language reflects this with specific terms for different family members based on age and relationship.

## Tips

- **Learn in pairs** - Older/younger sibling pairs
- **Practice with your family** - Try describing your family in Chinese
- **Notice patterns** - Some words repeat (爸爸, 妈妈)
- **Use context** - Family words often appear together

## Ready to Practice?

Use the flashcards to learn these essential family terms. You'll use them in conversations about yourself and others!
