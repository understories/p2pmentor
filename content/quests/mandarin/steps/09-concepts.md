# Day 9: Common Concepts

## Learning Essential Concepts

These are fundamental words that appear in almost every conversation. Learn adjectives, basic verbs, and essential concepts.

## Practice with Flashcards

Master these core concepts. They're building blocks for more complex sentences.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Concept Categories

You'll learn:
- **Adjectives**: good, big, small, many, few, new, old
- **Essential verbs**: to be, to have, to be at/in
- **Fundamental concepts**: used in almost every sentence

## Tips

- **Form descriptions** - "This is good" (这个好)
- **Express possession** - "I have..." (我有...)
- **Describe location** - "I am at home" (我在家)
- **Combine everything** - Use with previous vocabulary
