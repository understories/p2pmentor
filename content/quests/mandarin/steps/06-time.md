# Day 6: Time

## Learning Time Vocabulary

Time words help you talk about when things happen. Learn words for days, time periods, and time units.

## Practice with Flashcards

Master time-related vocabulary. These words are essential for scheduling and talking about your day.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Time Concepts

You'll learn:
- **Relative time**: today, tomorrow, yesterday, now
- **Time units**: hour, minute, year, month, day, week
- **Temporal markers**: essential for conversations

## Tips

- **Use in sentences** - "Today I..." (今天我...)
- **Practice scheduling** - "Tomorrow at 3 o'clock" (明天三点)
- **Combine with actions** - "Now I study" (现在我学习)
- **Daily practice** - Use these words every day

## Ready to Practice?

Time vocabulary helps you talk about your schedule and make plans. Start practicing with the flashcards!
