# Day 3: Family

## Learning Family Vocabulary

Family relationships are important in Spanish-speaking cultures. In this step, you'll learn essential family member vocabulary.

## Practice with Flashcards

Use the flashcards below to practice family words.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Tips for Learning

1. **Think about your own family** - Connect words to people you know
2. **Practice with sentences** - "Mi madre es..." (My mother is...)
3. **Note gender differences** - hermano (brother) vs hermana (sister)
4. **Review regularly** - Family words come up in many conversations
