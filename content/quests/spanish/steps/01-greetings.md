# Day 1: Greetings

## Learning Greetings

Greetings are the foundation of any conversation. In this step, you'll learn 10 essential greeting words and phrases in Spanish.

## Practice with Flashcards

Use the flashcards below to practice. Each card shows:
- **Spanish word** on the front
- **English translation** on the back

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Tips for Learning

1. **Focus on pronunciation** - Spanish has consistent pronunciation rules
2. **Practice daily** - Even 5 minutes helps build memory
3. **Say it out loud** - Speaking helps reinforce learning
4. **Review regularly** - Come back to these words tomorrow

## Vocabulary Overview

You'll learn:
- Basic greetings (hello, goodbye)
- Polite expressions (thank you, you're welcome)
- Apologies (sorry, it's okay)
- Time-specific greetings (good morning, good afternoon, good evening)
- Polite requests (please)
