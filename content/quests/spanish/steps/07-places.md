# Day 7: Places

## Learning Places Vocabulary

Knowing how to talk about locations helps you navigate and describe where you are or where you're going. In this step, you'll learn common places and locations in Spanish.

## Practice with Flashcards

Use the flashcards below to practice place-related words.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Tips for Learning

1. **Connect to real places** - Think of places you visit regularly
2. **Practice with directions** - "Voy a la escuela" (I'm going to school)
3. **Learn location words** - aquí (here), allí (there)
4. **Review regularly** - Place vocabulary helps in many situations
