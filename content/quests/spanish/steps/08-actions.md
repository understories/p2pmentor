# Day 8: Actions

## Learning Action Verbs

Verbs are the engine of sentences. In this step, you'll learn common action verbs in Spanish.

## Practice with Flashcards

## Tips for Learning

1. **Learn infinitive forms** - ir (to go), venir (to come)
2. **Practice with sentences** - "Yo voy a la escuela" (I go to school)
3. **Note irregular verbs** - Some verbs have irregular forms
4. **Review regularly** - Verbs are used in every sentence
