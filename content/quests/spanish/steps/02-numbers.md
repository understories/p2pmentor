# Day 2: Numbers

## Learning Numbers

Numbers are essential for everyday communication. In this step, you'll learn numbers 1-10 in Spanish.

## Practice with Flashcards

Use the flashcards below to practice counting from uno to diez.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Tips for Learning

1. **Count objects** - Practice by counting items around you
2. **Say numbers in sequence** - Practice counting up and down
3. **Use in context** - Try saying "tengo [número] años" (I am [number] years old)
4. **Review regularly** - Numbers are used constantly in conversation
