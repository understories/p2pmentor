# Day 4: Food

## Learning Food Vocabulary

Food is central to culture and daily life. In this step, you'll learn essential food and drink vocabulary in Spanish.

## Practice with Flashcards

Use the flashcards below to practice food-related words.

**Goal:** Review at least 8 cards to complete this step.

## Tips for Learning

1. **Visualize the items** - Picture each food as you learn
2. **Practice with meals** - "Para desayuno, como..." (For breakfast, I eat...)
3. **Learn verbs too** - comer (to eat), beber (to drink)
4. **Review regularly** - Food vocabulary is used daily
